TRENDING

Federal Student Aid Overhaul Announced for 2027
Financial Aid Policy Enrollment
Policy

Department of Education announces major restructuring of federal student aid programs, simplifying FAFSA to 15 questions and expanding Pell Grant eligibility to include part-time students. Changes expected to impact enrollment patterns across all institutions starting Fall 2027.

Key Insights

  • Simplified FAFSA may reduce application friction, potentially increasing aided student applications by 15-20%
  • Part-time Pell eligibility aligns with NEU's adult learner and CPS program strengths
  • Competitor institutions likely to adjust packaging strategies - need to monitor
Source: Chronicle of Higher Education | Jan 20, 2026

Boston University Launches New Global Scholars Program
Boston University Scholarships International
Competition

BU announces $30M scholarship initiative targeting high-achieving international students, offering full tuition plus stipends for top 100 admits from underrepresented countries. Program directly competes with similar offerings at peer institutions.

Key Insights

  • $30M investment signals BU's aggressive international recruitment strategy
  • Focus on underrepresented countries (Africa, Southeast Asia) overlaps with NEU target markets

INTERNATIONAL

UK Post-Study Work Visa Extended to 3 Years
UK Visa Policy International Students
Policy

UK government extends post-study work visa from 2 to 3 years for all degree levels, making UK increasingly competitive destination for international students. Policy change expected to significantly boost applications to UK universities, including NEU London campus.

Key Insights

  • 3-year work visa makes UK more attractive than US 1-year OPT for many students
  • Opportunity for NEU London campus to leverage this advantage in recruitment
  • May see some students choose UK pathway over US Boston campus
Source: The Guardian | Jan 20, 2026
